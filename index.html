Complete all 3 steps to start selling on ZÚLA. Verification takes 24–48 hours.

1 Government ID + Selfie

Upload a government-issued photo ID (passport, driver's licence, national ID) and a selfie holding the ID.

2 Business / Individual Registration

Sole traders upload proof of address. Registered businesses upload their certificate (CAC or equivalent).

🏢

Prove you own or have rights to sell what you're listing — purchase invoice, brand certificate, or creator declaration.
