Become a Verified Seller

Complete all 4 steps · Takes ~5 minutes · Review in 24–48 hrs

1
2

Step 1 of 4

🪪 Government-issued ID

Upload a clear photo of your passport, driver's licence, or national ID card. Both sides if applicable.

Accepted: Passport · Driver's licence · National ID · Voter's card
🪪
Tap to upload your ID
JPG, PNG or PDF · Max 10MB
💡 Tip: make sure all text and your photo are clearly visible. Blurry images will be rejected.
Step 2 of 4

🤳 Selfie with your ID

Take a photo of yourself holding the same ID document you just uploaded. Your face and the ID must both be clearly visible.

🤳
Tap to take or upload selfie
Face + ID in same photo · Good lighting
💡 Tip: hold the ID next to your face. Don't cover the ID number or your photo on the document.
Step 3 of 4

🏢 Proof of address or registration

Sole traders upload a utility bill or bank statement (less than 3 months old). Registered businesses upload a CAC certificate or equivalent.

Accepted: Utility bill · Bank statement · CAC certificate · Business licence
🏢
Tap to upload document
JPG, PNG or PDF · Max 10MB
💡 Tip: the document must show your full name and address. Redact your sort code if preferred.
Step 4 of 4

✅ Proof of product ownership

Prove you have the right to sell your products. This protects both buyers and sellers.

Accepted: Purchase invoice · Brand certificate · Creator declaration · Manufacturing record
📄
Tap to upload ownership proof
JPG, PNG or PDF · Max 10MB
📋 Before you submit, confirm:
By submitting, you confirm all information is accurate. False documents will result in a permanent ban.
