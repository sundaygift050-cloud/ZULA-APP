Seller Verification

ZÚLA requires every seller to pass 3 verification layers before listing anything. This is what makes our marketplace scam-free.

🪪
Layer 1 — Government ID + Face Selfie

Upload any government-issued ID from any country in the world. Then take a live selfie holding the ID. We use liveness detection — no static photos.

🌍 150+ countries accepted
🤖 AI liveness check
🔒 Encrypted storage
🏢
Layer 2 — Business or Individual Registration

Sole traders upload proof of address. Registered businesses upload their certificate (CAC, Companies House, EIN, or any country equivalent). Brands upload trademark registration.

👤 Individuals welcome
🏢 Any country's reg docs
